Why Make This Website

I made this website to showcase my skills, projects, and details that you don't have enough space to put on a resume. I also wanted to learn how to make a website from scratch and also deploy the website so anyone can access it. Doing this website also helped me learn how to do the basics HTML and CSS to make a website look good. Most importantly, the website shows that I am a self-starter and can learn new things on my own.

This website is simple by design and is meant to be a quick and easy way to get to know me and my work.

How to Navigate the Website

The website is simple to navigate. You can click on the links in the navigation bar to go to the different sections of the website. The navigation bar is at the top of the page and is always there so you can easily go to the different sections of the website.
